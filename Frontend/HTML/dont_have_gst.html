Don't Have a GST Number? Don't Worry !

Selling to millions? Now possible — even without a regular GSTIN.

For sellers not registered under GST, an Enrolment ID or UIN is required to proceed with the registration. Don’t have one yet? [Apply here] and continue your Sign-In on Bharat Bazaar.

Who Can Sell Without GST?

Individuals selling non-taxable goods
Resellers of unbranded items
Small businesses earning below ₹40 Lakhs annually
Sellers under Composition Scheme

What You Can Sell Without GST

Allowed Products

  • Handicrafts, Home Decor
  • Clothing (Unbranded)
  • Art & DIY supplies

Not Allowed

  • Branded electronics
  • Branded cosmetics
  • GST-taxable items

How to Start Selling Without GST

1️⃣ Sign up on Bharat Bazaar
2️⃣ Choose “I have Enrolment ID”
3️⃣ Add Your Details
4️⃣ Start listing your products
