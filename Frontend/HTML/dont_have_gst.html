Don't Have a GST Number? Don't Worry !

Selling to millions? Now possible — even without a regular GSTIN.

Start selling on Bharat Bazaar today—even without a GSTIN.

Who Can Sell Without GST?

Individuals selling non-taxable goods
Resellers of unbranded items
Small businesses earning below ₹40 Lakhs annually
Sellers under Composition Scheme

What You Can Sell Without GST

✅ Allowed Products

  • 🎨 Handicrafts, Home Decor
  • 👕 Clothing (Unbranded)
  • 🧵 Art & DIY supplies

❌ Not Allowed

  • 📱 Branded electronics
  • 💄 Branded cosmetics
  • 🧾 GST-taxable items

How to Start Selling Without GST

  1. Sign up on Bharat Bazaar
  2. Choose “I don’t have GST”
  3. Upload basic documents (Aadhar, PAN)
  4. Start listing your products
