Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to start selling on Bharat Bazaar?

To begin selling on Bharat Bazaar, you’ll need your PAN Card, Bank Account Details, GST Certificate (if applicable), and a Valid Address Proof for verification.

Are there any guidelines for listing my products on Bharat Bazaar?

All sellers must follow Bharat Bazaar’s product listing standards, including clear product images, accurate descriptions, pricing details, and authenticity proofs to ensure a high-quality shopping experience.

How long does it take to start selling on Bharat Bazaar?

Once you submit your documents and product details, onboarding typically takes 7 to 10 business days, depending on verification and catalog approval.

What is the payment cycle on Bharat Bazaar?

Bharat Bazaar follows a 7-day payment cycle from the date of successful order delivery, ensuring quick payouts for sellers.

Does Bharat Bazaar provide tools to manage orders?

Yes, Bharat Bazaar provides a free Seller Dashboard to track orders, manage inventory, update listings, and monitor earnings.

How does product delivery work on Bharat Bazaar?

Sellers can handle shipping themselves or use Bharat Bazaar’s reliable logistics partners to ensure smooth deliveries.

What support does Bharat Bazaar offer to help sellers grow?

We offer marketing assistance, platform-wide promotions, featured listings, and real-time insights to boost your brand visibility and sales.

How much can sellers grow with Bharat Bazaar's support?

Sellers using Bharat Bazaar’s promotional tools have experienced growth of up to 5X within the first few months, based on product quality and consistency.

Is there a registration fee to join Bharat Bazaar?

No, Bharat Bazaar follows a Zero Commission and No Registration Fee policy — allowing you to keep 100% of your profits with no hidden charges.
